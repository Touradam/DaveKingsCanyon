About the Land

A Rare Sierra Foothills Opportunity

The property sits in the Sierra Nevada foothills in Dunlap, an unincorporated community in Fresno County, California, at roughly 1,900 feet of elevation. It lies directly on Highway 180 — known as the “Gateway to the Sierras” and the start of the 50-mile Kings Canyon Scenic Byway — approximately 38 miles east of Fresno and only about 19–20 miles from the entrance to Sequoia & Kings Canyon National Parks (Grant Grove).

The area enjoys a warm, dry Mediterranean climate with hot sunny summers and mild winters, plus occasional foothill snow. Surrounded by spectacular mountain views, oak woodlands, and rolling terrain, it is close to the Giant Sequoia National Monument, the Kings River, Pine Flat Reservoir, and Wonder Valley — making it a natural basecamp for visitors heading to the national parks.

This combination of size, scenic beauty, highway frontage, and proximity to two national parks gives the land exceptional tourism and hospitality development potential.

Vision for the Land

Four distinct development directions illustrate the property’s versatility. Click any rendering to view it full size.

Secluded Campground

A campground where individual campsites are tucked into the natural brush and trees, screened and hidden from one another for maximum privacy and an immersive nature experience.

Glamping Resort

The same private, tucked-away layout, but instead of campers, beautiful private tented structures — each containing a king-sized bed and many of the amenities of a hotel room (including a private bathroom) — delivering luxury hospitality in the outdoors.

Grand Resort

A full-scale destination resort featuring a hotel, restaurant, swimming pool, pickleball courts, walking trails, and a conference center with ample parking.

A very small, five-space campground paired with a grand “caretaker’s residence” — an estate-style vacation home of roughly 12,000 square feet. Additional detail for this concept will be expanded in a future update.

A Canvas for Visionary Hospitality

Each concept leverages the same core strengths: privacy within oak woodlands, dramatic Sierra views, and a location that sits between Fresno’s metropolitan reach and the world-class draw of Sequoia & Kings Canyon National Parks.

Whether your vision is eco-tourism, upscale glamping, a full destination resort, a private estate with selective guest accommodations, corporate retreats, or gateway lodging for national park visitors, the property’s size and setting provide room to execute at a meaningful scale.
