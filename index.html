About the Land

Sierra Foothills. Highway Frontage. Park Country.

Set at the snowline of the Sierra Nevada foothills near Squaw Valley in unincorporated Fresno County, this parcel sits at roughly 4,500 feet of elevation right off of Highway 180. The route is known as the Gateway to the Sierras and is part of a 50-mile Kings Canyon Scenic Byway. Fresno is about 38 miles to the west. The entrance to Kings Canyon and Sequoia National Parks is only 7½ miles away.

The setting combines warm, dry Mediterranean summers with mild winters and occasional foothill snow. Oak woodlands, rolling terrain, and open mountain views surround the land. Nearby attractions include: The General Grant Grove of giant redwoods, which contains the ‘Nation’s Christmas Tree.’ The majestic Kings Canyon is one of the deepest canyons in the country and contains the Kings River, the Boyden Caverns, and the beautiful Cedar Grove. Sequoia National Park, which contains the world’s largest tree, the General Sherman, is only 36 miles away on the beautiful Generals Highway.

Rare recreational zoning, scenic character, direct highway frontage, and proximity to two national parks make this an outstanding candidate for a tourism and hospitality investment.

This sale includes three tax lots: 105 acres zoned recreational; 68 acres zoned recreational with a two-acre carve-out for the Snowline Lodge, which has a long-term lease until 2050; and 240 acres zoned RC 40, which allows for six home sites on 40 acres each.

Grand Resort

With over 800,000 visitors each year to Kings Canyon National Park, there is more than enough traffic to sustain a large destination resort. The National Park Service has removed the private lodges within the park and replaced them with rustic accommodations. This is the only private land zoned recreational that has all the necessary attributes for building a luxury complex.

This unique property has enough buildable land, miles of highway access, close proximity to the park entrance (7.5 miles), access to a known aquifer, and electrical power in place. There will be no significant nearby competition.

Glamping Resort

The same secluded layout, upgraded with private tent-style suites. Each unit offers a king bed, a private bath, and hotel-level comfort in an outdoor setting.

Campground

Individual campsites nest into brush and trees, screened from one another so guests enjoy privacy, quiet, and a direct connection to the landscape.

Combination: Small Campground + Luxury Caretaker’s Residence

If you are looking for a small, extremely manageable business combined with a luxury home, then this option is for you. Envision a small five-to-ten dry-site, self-service campground and your ‘caretaker’s residence,’ which has no legal limits for size. Dry camping means no electrical, water, or sewer hookups. All campers are self-contained for at least one night and sometimes simply need a level, safe place to park for the evening. The payment system can be either online or via a drop-box. No campfires would be allowed. The only maintenance would be routine cleaning around the sites and occasional trash pickup. The campground would sit near the highway entrance, and your caretaker’s residence could be situated anywhere else on the property, including atop the ridge overlooking the grand San Joaquin Valley.
